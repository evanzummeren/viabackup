Citizens Beyond Walls

 
Citizens Beyond Walls (CBW) is a European project that     targets making citizens active in order to oppose the   growth of Extreme right parties.

This happens through research, gathering material en stories and lobbying for initiatives against the alarming growth of extreme right groups. It's a project that lasts 16 months and started in december 2013. There are eight countries that participate: Italy, Hungary, The Netherlands, Catalony(Spain), Greece, Bulgaria, Serbia, Germany.

Check out all the info at www.citizensbeyondwalls.org

International project “Citizens Beyond Walls” 

Since December 2013, Stichting VIA is taking part in an EU Project called 'Citizens Beyond Walls' led  by SCI Italy. The projects' action, spans over 16 months and involves 8 EU countries (Italy, Hungary, the Netherlands, Greece, Bulgaria, Serbia, Spain and Germany).The project aims in activating European Citizens and fostering inclusive policies with particular focus on new citizens and minorities; whilst respecting Citizens Rights in Europe.

Citizens beyond walls summer workcamps: Peace weeks

As part of the project 8 special "Peace-Week" workcamp projects will be held in each of the participating 8 European countries in the summer of 2014: Greece, Spain, Serbia, Italy, Bulgaria, Hungary, The Netherlands, Germany

 

The aim of the project "Citizens Beyond Walls" is fostering inclusive policies to respect Citizens' rights in Europe, with specific focus on new citizens and minorities. Extreme right wing groups are a threat for peace in Europe, where peace is not only absence of conflict but the condition where citizens' are equally endowed of rights. "Citizens Beyond Walls", is an action aimed at activating European Citizens to counter the mentioned process.
