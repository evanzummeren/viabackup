VIA News

Welcome to the New Season!

The VIA season 2010 kicked off on March 15! We are excited with this year's selection of workcamps and volunteers, and eager for you to join us in any way you can!

There are currently spots available for all projects. But the sooner you register, the greater the chance that you are placed in your first choice of workcamp. Nearly all the summer projects can be viewed in the projectenoverzicht, but check back for updates!

Open Day Beckons You Closer

Please join us at our Open Day on March 20 for information on short and long-term volunteering (LTV) opportunities, or just to come chill with active VIA volunteers! Folders, films, books, people, coffee, tea, and international solidarity... if all goes well, we'll plant some bulbs in the garden. Read the full event description here.

The Officer Who Places: 2010

We happen to be sticking to our trend of Polish Placement Officers. This month, Magda will appropriate the VIA Amsterdam office — answering calls, encouraging volunteers, overseeing forms, and adding to the life of the seasonal party. After spending this last year in Croatia as a long term volunteer, Madga has plenty experience to offer, and we are happy to have her with us! Read it in Magda's own words here. Office is open from 2 to 5, and wednesdayevenings.

A Weekend of PR and Groups Fun

 

What: A weekend in which we will do the work that to do alone is tedious, boring and takes too much effort, but together is fun, effective and light as a feather. Dancing and singing is permitted. For details visit this page.

Better, Faster, Smarter, Smoother

SCI — the 'mother-organisation' of VIA — has completely revised and updated the Online Placement System for this year! The new user-interface, redesigned with the Joomla OpenSource Website Management System, is more intuitive and with enhanced performance. It should make many VIA volunteers' lives run more smoothly! Read more from Huub.

SCI Awarded "Member of the Month"

SCI was named the United Network of Young Peacebuilders member of the month! Visit the UNOY website to read the full article (PDF).
