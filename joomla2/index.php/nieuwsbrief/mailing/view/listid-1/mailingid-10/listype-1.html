Preparation Weekend

So if you have already been placed in a summer project, you have received your invitation to the Preparation Weekend to be held on June 12 and 13. Like the name says, during this weekend we will help you prepare for your project, with information and inspiration.

For those who haven’t applied yet, but are thinking about it, this weekend is an opportunity to get an idea of how a volunteer project works. So feel free to join, just let us know you are coming!

The Preparation Weekend will take place this year at De Grote Pyr. The Grote Pyr is a living and working community that is located in the center of The Hague.

Office Space

VIA Netherlands and Women’s Global Network for Reproductive Rights share an office together. We are looking for a third party to share the office and rent with, as of today.The loft offers space for approximately three work stations. Furthermore, we share a meeting table, a kitchen and coordinate practicalities. The rent is €150,- per month, and the office is located in a beautiful area of Amsterdam, near the centre and easy to reach. Interested? Contact Huub@stichtingvia.nl
