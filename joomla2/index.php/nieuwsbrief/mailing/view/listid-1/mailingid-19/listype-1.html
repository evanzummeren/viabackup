Freedom not fronted in Amsterdam

Last 15-16 february VIA participated in the  organization of the 5th international meeting Freedom not frontex, a project for making a march next may from Strasbourg to Bruxelles to claim the asylum´s seekers rights. Read the full article at our web or visit http://freedomnotfrontex.noblogs.org

  Citizens beyond walls  

  

This year, VIA is also involved in a European project called Citizens beyond walls (CBW), that makes citizens actives in order to oppose the extreme right parties in Europe, through research, gathering material en stories and lobbying for initiatives against the alarming growth of extreme right groups. Get more info at our web: http://stichtingvia.nl/index.php/citizens-beyond-walls

 

Call to Action:

 

From 15th until 23rd of March is European Action Week Against Racism and in this context, a large demonstration against racism will be organised in Amsterdam on Saturday the 22nd of March.

 

Since VIA Netherlands is currently participating in a project aimed at opposing extreme right wing growth in Europe, Citizens Beyond Walls, this demonstration would be a fit opportunity to ask demonstrators and by-standers about their motivations and concerns. The results of these short street interviews will be used in our research for the project. We are looking for enthusiastic volunteers to help us with these interviews. If you have the time and the interest,  please send an e-mail to alexandra@stichtingvia.nl.

 

  VIA supporting NL DOET 2014

The next 22nd of march VIA is going to join a new NL Doet edition, the largest voluntary action of Netherlands. The orange fonds let therefore with for NL Doet see how important active citizens for the society.

 Sign so at chris@stichtingvia.nl your job or get involved as a volunteer. Participate in NL-Doet with VIA...
