VIA supports the national volunteering days on March 16/17

The platform NL DOET brings volunteers and organizations in need for volunteers together on March 16th and 17th. Obviously VIA likes this idea, because we basically do the same on an international scale. So when NL does, VIA does too!

This year some VIA volunteers will be helping a hand. Hereby we call out for you to join us! At www.nldoet.nl you can find and apply for a project in your city. For those interested in a project in Groningen the 17th of March: Tanja is gathering volunteers to join a project together.

Tanja on Wieringen

That is me in the picture, presenting the sweat lodge we built that we were just about to inaugurate. Native Americans have sweat lodge ceremonies for different kinds of spiritual healings. I can assure you this was a very special experience. It takes quite an effort in mental preparation to get a group of international volunteers (semi) naked in a spiritual sauna. But almost everybody joint the first of three healing round in the hot lodge we built together.
