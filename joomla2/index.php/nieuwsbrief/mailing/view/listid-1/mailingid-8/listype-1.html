Looking at a New Season

The new season begins March 15!! While our LTVs are headed to Asia and the US, back home in Holland the plate of project offerings is about to be served! Follow this link for some updates from Floor and Marion.

Meanwhile, VIA is searching for coordinators of workcamps in the Netherlands. If you have some experience and are curious about the possibilities for 2010, write to floor(at)stichtingvia.nl.

In other news, we have chosen a new Polish Placement Officer: Magdalena Nowicka! Also, workcamp guides are being replaced with a more eco-friendly brochure, available in March. See you at Open Day Weekend mid-March!
 
Sjoerd's Goodbye
Sjoerd could almost surely be found in the VIA office, in the VIA kitchen, and at all the VIA meetings and gatherings. If you didn't know when, you could call Sjoerd. If you didn't know how, you could shoot him an email. Reliable, honest, a listener and explorer — Sjoerd has decided to focus his energy on something new: peak oil and climate change. These are his goodbye words... We will miss you, Sjoerd.
 
A Palestinian Feast
Mujadderah, mtabbel, hummus, tabouleh, salata bakdoensiyye; volunteers worked for hours wrapping wineleaves around a filling... Just to get a taste for the delicious selection at VIA's first official dinner party: the Palestinian Supper! Read Lokien's account of the evening's festivities.
