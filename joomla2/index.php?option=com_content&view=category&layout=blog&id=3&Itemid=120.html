Camp of the week

Call for Peaceweek in Athens

Call for the Peace next edition in Athens. The Peace Week will consist of three different stages. First, the volunteers will participate in the Anti-Racism Festival of Athens, together with the team of SCI Hellas; then, pulling from our experiences in the festival and in our lives, we will create an artistic performance, combining theatrical parts with music, costumes or whatever fits our creativity, all together – a piece that speaks about peace, diversity, anti-discrimination and, ultimately, about humankind; to finish this work, we will present this itinerary work of art in the streets of Athens to sensitize people and spread the idea of peace.

You can be one of the 14 participants from Netherlands that can participate in the peace weeks in Europe. 55% of the travel costs (up to 200 euro) as well as food and accommodation are covered.

Check it out all the info at http://citizensbeyondwalls.org/agenda/peace-week-in-athens/

Peace week in Italy: `La città dell´utopia´

“La Città dell’Utopia” (Utopia City- www.lacittadellutopia.it) is a project of SCI Italy that takes place in Rome. The project is an ongoing workshop on active citizenship and local development. There are different events, courses and workshops organized around 3 main themes: interculturality, sustainable lifestyles and active citizenship. “La Città dell’Utopia” will organize, together with social organisations in Rome, a public event connected with the european project “Citizens Beyond Walls” (see below) and one week workcamp called “Peace Week”. The public event will focus on Freedom of Movement, not only as a no border concept and the right to migrate, but also as a basic principle (a fundamental right) on which we claim human, social, civil and political rights within a Country.

How to apply?

Search peace week in the browser of SCI home page (www.workcamps.info) and follow the instructions, or visit http://citizensbeyondwalls.org/agenda/peace-week-italy/

Peaceweek in Greece

 

The Peace Week in Athens is one of 8 Peace Weeks organized in framework of ‘Citizens Beyond Walls’ project, which focuses on rise of extreme rise movements and parties in Europe. The aim of the project is raising awareness of Athenian society on issues of multicultural diversity, nationality and inclusion.

The study theme will be focused on the issues of peace and diversity. It will also approach the subject of Citizens Beyond Walls, through an overview of the research going on in Greece about the extreme-right movements and how to counteract their influence on society.

 

For more info visit: 

http://citizensbeyondwalls.org/peace-weeks/

Join the first PEACE WEEK in Sofia

The Peace Week in Sofia is one of eight Peace Weeks organized within the framework of 'Citizens Beyond Walls' project, which focuses on rise of extreme right wing movements and parties in Europe. The aim of the project is raising awareness in Bulgarian society on issues of diversity, nationality and inclusion. Because the extreme right wing parties in Bulgaria seem particularly interested in refugees and asylum-seekers, this topic will be addressed specifically during this peace week. The peace week will be organized by CVS-Bulgaria.
